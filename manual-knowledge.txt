## ADMISSIONS PROCESS
### Engineering (École d'Ingénierie — Nouaceur campus):
   FR: Accessible avec un Bac scientifique ou technique, sur dossier et entretien.
       Pour postuler ou en savoir plus : https://www.mundiapolis.ma/contact
   EN: Open to students with a scientific or technical Bac, via application file and
       interview. To apply or learn more: https://www.mundiapolis.ma/contact

### For 1st year (Bac+0) — all schools:
- Must hold Moroccan or foreign Baccalauréat
- Process: File review + oral interview with admissions jury
- Contact admissions: admissions@mundiapolis.ma / 00 212 529 01 37 07

### For 2nd or 3rd year (Bac+1 or Bac+2):
- Must hold BAC+1 or BAC+2
- Process: File review (previous institution, modules, grades) + motivation interview

### For Master's:
- Must hold BAC+3 or Licence degree

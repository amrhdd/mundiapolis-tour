The Project

A campus is more than buildings. It's the feeling of standing in a courtyard, deciding where to go next.

Most prospective students judge a university from a brochure or a pin on a map. We wanted something closer to the truth — a way to actually move through Mundiapolis, room by room, from anywhere in the world.

So we built one. A 360° tour powered by Marzipano, paired with Amira, an AI guide who knows the campus and answers in English, French, or Arabic — whichever you speak to her in.
